D2rPlus is a diablo 2 resurrected mod that adds to the game in the way of things to do and items to wear and use.
At least that was what it started as where it goes is dependant on users of the mod.

The items are based on a D2LOD release collection from Talonrage.
Its a shame that most of the online links are now dead however the complete plugin system is still available at the The Phrozen Keep.
I have used this system within my own personal mod since leaving the bnet world in early 1.10 as open dueling was basically screwed over by 1.10.
I like the stuff done by Talonrage becouse it was thought out to be balanced within the game.
So on the release of Resurrected thought i would update my own files, then since nothing was similar i decided to release this mod.
It may or may not last depends on the interest.
Absolute full credit to Talonrage not only for putting those packages together for people to use but for doing a great job of it.
This may not be as good but i am trying :)

There are alot of ways this mod can go and maybe it will but there will be others that will get themselves back into it over time and mods will be falling from the servers allover.
The ability to mod D2 is what kept it alive so it would be a shame to see it not continue.
